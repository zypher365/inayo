Frequently Asked Questions

General


What is Inayo?

Inayo is India’s #1 hyperlocal marketplace for pharma and healthcare products. It lets you order Prescription medicines, OTC medicines, Baby Care, Personal Care, Ayurvedic, Herbal and other health care products that you need every day from our website/mobile app.

How do I order via Inayo?

The flow is pretty simple.

  • For prescription medicines, just upload the image/scan.
  • Check out the cart with everything you need
  • Your order will be delivered by a nearby pharmacy within 90 minutes

    • What kind of products do you sell?

    On Inayo mobile app, choose from over 30,000 products spread across various categories such as medicines, Ayurvedic products, Sports nutrition, personal care products, baby care products and much more.

    Which cities and locations do you operate in?

    Inayo currently operates in Powai, Mumbai

    What is the minimum order value?

    There is no minimum order value. For orders below ₹100, we charge a ₹30 delivery charge.

    How will I know if any item in my order is unavailable?

    We try to make sure our inventory is always updated. We never go out of stock as we have multiple pharmacies in each area. If we really go out of stock, we will suggest a replacement for you. Rest assured about payments, in case we are unable to find the item for you, we will initiate the refund immediately.

    How can I make changes to my order before and after confirmation?

    You can edit your order in the cart before check out. In case you want to change something in the order after it has been placed, you can write to us at info@inayo.in or simply call us at 9833232676 between 9AM and 9PM.
